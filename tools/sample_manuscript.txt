Title: A Study on the Influence and Effect of Temperature on Cell Growth

Abstract
In this paper, we mainly studied and investigated the influence and effect that the temperature has on the growth of the cells, and we found that the higher the temperature is, the faster the cells will grow, which is a very good and important result that can help a lot in real applications. The experiment is conducted in 2023 with the help of a number of advanced devices in order to make use of the most accurate measurement, and it is worth noting that the result shows proposed method achieves best performance compared with other method that are used by other researchers in the process of their works.
